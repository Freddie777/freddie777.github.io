Gothic II

Gothic 2 is one of my all time favorite games. Because of the popularity of the 'Elder Scrolls: Morrowind' Gothic 2 went largely unnoticed at the time. But it seems to be very popular on gog.com today.

Things that make Gothic 2 special:

  • Its large yet detailed world.
  • The custom designed NPCs and their personality.
  • The music

Things that are most annoying in Gothic 2 orderd worst to least:

  1. The cluncky controls.
  2. The dragon battles.
  3. No real fast travel.

At worldofgothic there is still an active fan community for the Gothic games.
